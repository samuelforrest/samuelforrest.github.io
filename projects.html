NEA: Stock Simulator Mobile Application for Beginners

As part of my A-Level Computer Science course, I’m developing a custom-built software application from scratch as my Non-Exam Assessment (NEA). It includes real-time-inspired price changes, portfolio tracking, and detailed performance feedback. The aim is to combine coding, problem-solving, and real-world finance concepts into a functional and educational tool — perfect for students or beginners looking to explore investment basics. 🚀📊
